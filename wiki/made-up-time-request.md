Made Up Time Request procedure

*Staff member creates a copy of the [Made Up Time Request](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1DZT7cC3bsyzgyytKjghdFMgzzBJ2Jk6cmFN3JZIsdOI/edit?gid=0#gid=0) master sheet. Fill in your created copy and sends it to Kelly via Slack or email
*Kelly asks Suzanne to approve

*If declined Kelly advises staff member with reason why

*If approved, Kelly confirms via slack or email to the staff member

*Kelly adds the made up time as an absence into Citation with a copy of the form

*Kelly adds the time off into Teamwork calender as an 'Unavailable' Event, with the title 'Staff Member - time off'

*Kelly adds an Event 'Made up time' to Teamwork Calendar with the title 'Staff member made up time' adding a note in the event description of related time off date

*Once the team member has made their time up they should email Kelly to confirm time has been made up (we may need to adjust the Company policy)
Kelly Updates the Teamwork events to confirm time was made up.

*Any where the team member hasnt made up time as agreed should have time marked as unpaid and Kelly should notify the staff member
