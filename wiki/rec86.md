# Pitch
Rich Results are a key factor in successful Ecommerce growth, playing a key part in the effectiveness of both organic and paid campaigns for products. Magento 2 supports basic product schema but in order to achieve greater coverage and product data attributes for all product types, we have developed this work package to improve exposure to cover all known attributes. This work package covers simple, configurable and grouped products.
