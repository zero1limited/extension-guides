# Sub Category List
Your content here


## Pitch

Almost all merchants have multiple category layers on their website, and most often it is more appropriate at higher levels, to show visual subcategories rather than directly to a product list. This task includes implementation of our custom code to achieve this since it is not a core Magento feature
