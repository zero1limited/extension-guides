Checking for Pending Hols on Atlas Citation

Once as week - check if any staff members have requested any holidays

Login to Atlas Citation

Once logged in - follow the steps in this [short video](https://share.vidyard.com/watch/VLbtvBT7u9uXh6uwYz9n2H)

Detailed steps - 

*Go to the menu My Area > Calendar down the left hand side

*Make sure the employee box above the calendar is empty

*Go through the calendar and see if any pending hols have been requested. If you hover over the dates where it states team bookings you are looking for any that are coloured orange. Orange details pending hols not yet authorised

*Check through the remaining months left of the year 

*Detail any staff members and the Employee, Date requested, Duration of hol and if anyone else is off at that same time

*Slack this to Suzanne for her to approve or add additional comments for the requested holiday

*Once Suzanne has stated that the holiday is authorised

Go to left hand side menu bar again Employees/Workers

Click Manage and you will have a list of current employees

Locate the employee you are wanting to see holidays for and click the view option on the right hand side of their record
