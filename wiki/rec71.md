# Testing

You obviously get a score for accessibility on the Lighthouse report (red, yellow, green). Quickest way is to look at that once you've made your changes to see what difference it's made.

The more substantial way is Google Search Console. In there, you'll find errors / warnings about accessibility. You can ask Google to rescan the site once your changes are live to confirm in there that the errors / warnings are gone.
