# Where Teams are created/managed

| Task                    | Where to Do It                      | Notes                                                               |
| ----------------------- | ----------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Create and manage teams | **Tempo → Teams**                   | Add members, assign team leads, link projects                       |
| Track time by team      | Tempo Timesheets                    | Team time appears in reports if members log time on assigned issues |
| Plan capacity           | Tempo Planner (if used)             | Teams must exist in Tempo for planning to work                      |
| Assign issues           | Jira (via assignee or custom field) | Can use **custom field** for “Team” if needed                       |
| Control permissions     | Jira Project Roles / Groups         | Doesn’t affect Tempo visibility                                     |

# Teams
- Alpha - Dedicated retainer Projects
- Team Beta - Dedicated retainer Projects
- Pool team - For team members who service all quoted projects, Internal R&D etc. Anything other than retainer work.

Admin will need to allocate any new project to the relevant team in Tempo Teams
