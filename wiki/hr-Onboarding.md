## Personnel details
- Make a copy of and Ask employee to complete [New Starter form](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1fbdZXt4ZIfICmr9WC1OMHNYuU0Q3CK5rhgH2BW1PBAg/edit?tab=t.0#heading=h.gjdgxs) - Upload to Google Drive personnel folder and to Atlas
- Ask for a copy of any qualifications and right to work in the UK documents, P45
- Add new employee to the bulk payroll account group
- Make a copy of [Contract of Employment](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1e5hPyInoS20xIDVW_21I45bn9292o2on/edit) Add to Google folder, add correct info and send it to the new employee - Once signed, upload copy to Atlas and Google personnel folder




## Guides
- Share company policies via Atlas; Staff handbook, Quality Policy, Company heirachy

# Week 1: 
## Orientation & Introduction
- Welcome meeting with HR Manager (Day 1)
- Overview of company values, mission, culture
- Explain the org structure and Team Set up
- Assign a 'Buddy'

## Introduce tools & Processes: 
- Walk them through how we use:
- Jira
- Confluence
- Slack
- Google calendar
- Tempo time tracking
- Share internal standards (QA Processes, design systems, coding guidelines)

## Assign initial tasks
- Small, non critical tasks or shadowing

# Weeks 2 -4: Integration & Contribution
## Build confidence
- Set up training sessions on products, platforms, or services.
- Schedule regular check-ins (e.g. weekly 1:1s with their manager).
- Give early feedback, focusing on strengths and opportunities to improve.
- Include them in planning and backlog grooming
